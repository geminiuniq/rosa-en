## Design Context

### Users
A Singapore Secondary-4 student preparing for the O-Level exams (English 1184, E-Maths 4048), aiming for **A1**. Uses the app **daily for long study sessions** — reading dense passages, working through practice questions, reviewing a wrong-answer notebook. A parent (the account owner) sets it up. Primary device: laptop at a desk, mostly **daytime**, some evening. Bilingual 中文/English throughout.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **calm · encouraging · focused**. A patient study companion under exam pressure — never loud, never gamified-childish, never sterile-corporate. It should feel like a well-made study journal you return to every day, not a flashy edtech dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Warm paper (护眼浅色).** Cream/warm-off-white surfaces, soft warm-dark ink (never pure black on pure white — that glare is the #1 eye-strain complaint for long reading). Muted, desaturated accents. Subject identity carried by a calm accent hue: English = ink-blue, Math = clay/terracotta; sage-green for "correct/mastered". Reference feel: Literata/e-reader reading mode, a Muji notebook, an exam-prep workbook printed on cream stock. Anti-references: dark mode with glowing neon accents, high-contrast white-on-black, purple→blue gradients, heavy drop-shadows.

### Design Principles
1. **护眼 first** — warm cream ground (~oklch 0.96), soft warm-ink text (~oklch 0.30, not #000), generous line-height (1.7+) and reading measure (≤72ch). No pure black/white; no bright-on-dark.
2. **Muted, not neon** — accents at moderate chroma; color is a rare 10% accent, neutrals do the work. Green means correct, red is restrained.
3. **Calm hierarchy** — hierarchy through size/weight/space, not loud color or heavy borders. No colored side-stripes, no gradient text, minimal shadows.
4. **Reading comfort** — content sized for a teen reading for an hour: base ≥16.5px, comfortable leading, no all-caps body.
5. **Offline-honest** — self-contained single-file apps; curated system font stack (warm serif for reading content, clean sans for UI chrome) so it degrades perfectly with no network.
